Near-IR Wavefront Sensor - Basic Parameters

Basic Parameters
Location Nasmyth focus (IR)
Spectral coverage of science path 0.6-2.4 um
Number of control elements 188
Deformable mirror Bimorph mirror with 188 control electrodes
High-order WFS NIR Pyramid Wavefront sensor with HgCdTe detector.
Control bandwidth > 100 Hz, 500 to 2000 corrections/sec
NGS magnitude -3 < H < 11
Off-axis steering 20 arcsec diameter
Wavelength range 0.95-1.8 um (y, J and H-band)

Modes supported in S24A

SCExAO

The nearIR WFS can be used with the following SCExAO modes :
  • CHARIS low-red broadband
  • CHARIS hi-red J, H or K-band
  • CHARIS PDI
  • VAMPIRES imaging
  • VAMPIRES PDI
  • MEC y and J band
  • REACH

The nearIR WFS cannot be used with fast polarization imaging (FPDI) FPDI cannot be co-scheduled with nearIR WFS as switching between the two modes required daytime instrument reconfiguration.

IRD

The NearIR WFS cannot be used with standard IRD operation (light extracted within AO188)

REACH operation (light extracted within SCExAO) is supported.

IRCS

The nearIR WFS can be used with the following IRCS modes :
  • 20 mas imaging mode with Y- , J- , H- , and K - band
  • Echelle mode with zJ- , J- , H- , and K - band

The nearIR WFS is incompatible with IRCS operation in L and M bands, and is incompatible with IRCS-polarimaetry
